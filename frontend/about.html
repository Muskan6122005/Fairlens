Methodology

How FairLens Detects Bias

📐 Disparate Impact Analysis

Measures whether different demographic groups receive significantly different positive outcomes. If a group's positive rate is less than 80% of the most-favored group, it triggers a flag.

⚖️ Demographic Parity

Checks that the probability of a positive outcome is equal across all demographic groups, regardless of other features. A key metric in EU AI Act compliance.

🔀 Proxy Variable Detection

Identifies columns that may act as proxies for sensitive attributes — e.g., zip code correlating with race, or job title correlating with gender.

Legal Landscape

Laws That Regulate AI Bias

🇮🇳 DPDP Act 2023 (India)

Digital Personal Data Protection Act mandates responsible AI processing. Violations can attract fines up to ₹250 Crore.

🇪🇺 EU AI Act

World's first comprehensive AI regulation. High-risk AI systems (hiring, credit, healthcare) must undergo mandatory bias audits. Fines up to €30M or 6% of global revenue.

🇺🇸 ECOA & Fair Housing Act

Equal Credit Opportunity Act prohibits discriminatory lending. Violations attract massive class-action lawsuits and regulatory fines.
